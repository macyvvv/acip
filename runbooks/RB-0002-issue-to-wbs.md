# RB-0002: Issue to WBS

## Objective

Convert approved issues into executable WBS without Human handholding.

## Steps

1. Identify issue objective.
2. Confirm Current Objective alignment.
3. Define scope and out of scope.
4. Decompose into tasks.
5. Assign owner by role.
6. Define Done conditions.
7. Add validation path.
8. Escalate only if approval or risk acceptance is required.

## Human Boundary

Human does not decompose tasks manually.

## Done Condition

WBS exists and can be executed by Codex.
