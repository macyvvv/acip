# 022 Asset Completion Policy

## Conclusion

Canonical Asset Production is complete only when asset definition, lifecycle, registry, production operations, quality, ROI, risk, and completion checks all exist and pass validation.

## Completion Criteria

Canonical Asset Production is complete when:

- Canonical Asset definition exists
- Asset production policy exists
- Quality Gate exists
- Lifecycle control exists
- Repository conventions exist
- Registry control exists
- Traceability control exists
- Intake policy exists
- Production workflow exists
- Review cadence exists
- Output policy exists
- Quality policy exists
- ROI policy exists
- Risk policy exists
- Completion checklist exists
- Validation scripts pass
- GitHub Actions checks exist
- Runtime implementation remains out of scope

## Completion Rule

Repository overrides conversation.

Current Objective is not complete until Repository evidence exists and Human approves closure.
