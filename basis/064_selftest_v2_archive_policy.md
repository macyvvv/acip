# 064 SelfTest v2 Archive Policy

## Conclusion

Archived files preserve historical reasoning and must be excluded from canonical duplicate, orphan, and drift checks.

## Rules

- `archive/**` is historical space.
- Archive content may duplicate ADR or WBS numbers.
- Archive content may be unreferenced.
- Archive content may contain stale terms.
- Archive content must still be scanned for secrets.

## Repository Rule

Repository overrides conversation.
