# 042 Execution Contract Policy

## Conclusion

Every autonomous or semi-autonomous execution must have an Execution Contract before Codex or future approved automation performs work.

## Contract Rule

- Repository overrides conversation.
- Current Objective must not change without approval.
- Human handles Mission, Approval, Emergency Stop.
- Codex executes only approved scope.
- ChatGPT reviews priority, risk, and scope.
- Runtime implementation remains out of scope until explicitly approved.
