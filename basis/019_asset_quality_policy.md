# 019 Asset Quality Policy

## Conclusion

Canonical Assets must pass a quality policy before they are treated as reusable source knowledge.

## Quality Dimensions

- Objective Fit
- Source Clarity
- Scope Control
- Reusability
- Traceability
- Risk Visibility
- Maintenance Cost
- ROI Link
- Strategic Fit
- Operational Usability

## Quality Rules

- Repository overrides conversation.
- Quality Gate must be completed before Human Approval.
- Quality Gate status must be recorded in the Asset Registry.
- Failed quality gates must block canonical merge unless explicitly waived by Human.
- Waivers must preserve reason and risk.
- Runtime implementation remains out of scope.

## Minimum Passing Standard

A Canonical Asset must:

1. state its objective
2. define scope and out of scope
3. preserve source context
4. identify intended reuse
5. identify risk notes
6. link to registry entry
7. link to related ADR/WBS when applicable
8. include revenue or value linkage
