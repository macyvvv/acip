# Video Format

## Short-Form Structure

- 0.0–0.8s → Extreme close-up, face dominant
- 0.8–2.0s → Slight zoom out
- 2.0–6.0s → Minimal action
- 6.0–10.0s → One-line unresolved text
- 10.0–12.0s → Silence / stillness

## Rules

- No interaction dependency
- No explanation
- Maintain ambiguity
- Keep motion minimal and deliberate
