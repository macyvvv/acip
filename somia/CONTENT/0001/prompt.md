## Animation Instruction

Within the first second, a weak/subtle chromatic-aberration glitch flicker plays — brief and understated, just enough to feel "off," not dramatic. She blinks once, then turns her head to directly face the camera/viewer — slowly and gradually over several seconds, unhurried, a deliberate reveal (not turning away, not showing the back of her head). Once facing the viewer, her eye contact stays brief and incidental, under a second, not sustained or intense, before she drifts back toward her original pose. Hair stays almost completely still throughout, no wind or swaying. Keep the illustrated style throughout the whole clip; do not drift toward photorealism.

## Camera Instruction

Start in a medium close-up at eye level, hold steady, no sudden zoom or dolly — quiet and unresolved, not tense.
