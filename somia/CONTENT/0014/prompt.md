## Animation Instruction

She remains fixed on the monitor throughout, posture almost entirely still. A faint cursor-blink light flickers across her eyes partway through the clip as she leans forward by only a few millimeters, absorbed in the screen, never turning toward the camera. Hair and fabric stay essentially motionless, no wind. Keep the illustrated style consistent throughout.

## Camera Instruction

Open on an extreme close-up of her eyes catching the monitor glow, slight zoom out to reveal her seated posture, then hold static and close, no further movement.
