# Prompt 0027

## Image Prompt (KV)

masterpiece, best quality, highly detailed illustration, 1girl, solo, standing at the edge of a wooden pier above shallow surf, bright coastal daylight, light ocean-blue and white and silver color palette, small wave washing over bare feet, natural-material anklet with tiny shell charms, sheer light-fabric dress hem lifting slightly, faint gentle smile, calm unreactive expression, gaze lowered toward water then lifting to the sea, not looking at viewer, high-key natural lighting, soft reflective shallow water backdrop, clear silhouette, illustration

## Negative Prompt

photorealistic, photo, 3d, western cartoon, disney, pixar, low quality, worst quality, bad anatomy, blurry, jpeg artifacts, suggestive pose, dark moody lighting, direct sustained eye contact, exaggerated expression, crying, hoodie, sportswear, night scene

## Animation Instruction

Open on an extreme close-up of her faint smile, gaze already lowered toward the water's edge. Zoom out slightly to reveal the pier and shallow surf. A small wave washes over her feet; she registers only the faintest, almost imperceptible reaction, then slowly lifts her gaze back to the open sea. Hold the seaward gaze through the remaining beats as the water recedes.

## Camera Instruction

Static medium-wide shot at a slight low angle, slow minimal zoom-out; brief incidental framing of the water at her feet before returning focus to her face, no cutting.
