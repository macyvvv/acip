# Prompt 0026

## Image Prompt (KV)

masterpiece, best quality, highly detailed illustration, 1girl, solo, seated on a bright window seat, sheer white curtain shifting in a soft draft, light ocean-blue and white and silver color palette, shaft of sunlight crossing her face, natural-material ring with small stone, sheer light-fabric dress, calm reassuring expression, eyes gently closed transitioning to softly open, gaze toward the window light, not looking at viewer, high-key natural lighting, soft airy interior backdrop, clear silhouette, illustration

## Negative Prompt

photorealistic, photo, 3d, western cartoon, disney, pixar, low quality, worst quality, bad anatomy, blurry, jpeg artifacts, dark moody lighting, direct sustained eye contact, exaggerated expression, crying, hoodie, sportswear, cluttered background, night scene

## Animation Instruction

Open on an extreme close-up of her calm face with eyes gently closed, sunlight already touching her skin. Zoom out slightly to reveal the window seat and shifting curtain. A shaft of light crosses her face as the curtain moves; she holds her closed eyes a beat longer, then slowly opens them toward the window, never toward the viewer. Settle into the open-eyed gaze on the window through the final beats.

## Camera Instruction

Static medium close-up at eye level with a slow, minimal zoom-out; soft natural light motivates a gentle brightness shift across the frame, no camera movement beyond the zoom-out.
