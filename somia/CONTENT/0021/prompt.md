# Prompt 0021

## Image Prompt (KV)

masterpiece, best quality, highly detailed illustration, 1girl, solo, sitting by an open window, bright high-key daylight, light ocean-blue and white and silver color palette, sheer white curtain billowing softly, wind-blown light hair strands, natural-material hairpin with small shell motif, loose sheer light-fabric dress, gentle smile, calm expression, gaze directed outward toward the sky through the window, not looking at viewer, hand raised lightly to tuck hair behind ear, soft airy lighting, clear silhouette against a bright sky backdrop, illustration

## Negative Prompt

photorealistic, photo, 3d, western cartoon, disney, pixar, low quality, worst quality, bad anatomy, blurry, jpeg artifacts, dark moody lighting, graphite tones, direct sustained eye contact, smirking, exaggerated expression, crying, hoodie, sportswear, cluttered background, night scene

## Animation Instruction

Open on an extreme close-up of her face already turned toward the window light, a faint gentle smile settled and unmoving. Zoom out slightly to reveal the open window and stirring curtain. A soft draft lifts the curtain and her hair; she raises one hand and tucks a strand behind her ear in a small, unhurried gesture, gaze never leaving the sky. Hold the final pose without further movement through the text and silence beats.

## Camera Instruction

Static camera at a slight low angle, extreme close-up easing into a medium shot with a slow, minimal zoom-out; no pan, no dolly, stays quiet throughout.
