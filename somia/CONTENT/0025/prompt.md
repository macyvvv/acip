# Prompt 0025

## Image Prompt (KV)

masterpiece, best quality, highly detailed illustration, 1girl, solo, standing at a bright shoreline, open sea stretching to the horizon, light ocean-blue and white and silver color palette, hair lifting gently in sea breeze, natural-material anklet with small stone beads, sheer light-fabric dress, quiet resolute expression, calm steady posture, gaze fixed on the distant horizon, not looking at viewer, high-key daylight, soft reflective water and sky backdrop, clear silhouette, illustration

## Negative Prompt

photorealistic, photo, 3d, western cartoon, disney, pixar, low quality, worst quality, bad anatomy, blurry, jpeg artifacts, dark moody lighting, direct sustained eye contact, exaggerated expression, crying, smiling widely, hoodie, sportswear, night scene

## Animation Instruction

Open on an extreme close-up of her quietly resolute face, gaze already fixed on the horizon. Zoom out slightly to reveal the shoreline setting. She takes one long, slow exhale; her hair lifts briefly with the released breath and the sea breeze, then settles. Her gaze never leaves the horizon through any beat. Hold the final still pose without further movement.

## Camera Instruction

Static wide shot at eye level with a slow, minimal zoom-out; camera remains fixed on her profile facing the sea, never crossing to a frontal angle.
