## Animation Instruction

She stays collapsed over the desk, face turned to one side, mostly motionless. Midway through the clip a notification icon pulses faintly at the screen edge; her fingers tap the desk twice near her mug in a small, quiet response, then go still again. She never lifts her head or looks toward the camera. Hair remains nearly static, no wind. Maintain the illustrated style throughout.

## Camera Instruction

Open on an extreme close-up of her face against her folded arms, slight zoom out to reveal the desk and monitors, then hold static and low, no further movement.
