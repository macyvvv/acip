# Prompt 0059

## Image Prompt (KV)

masterpiece, best quality, highly detailed illustration, 1girl, solo, warm cream and amber color palette, softly blurred warm domestic room, warm side light, loosely falling bun with escaped strands, crescent hairpin, body turned slightly to the side, arm partway extended toward something off-frame, hand withdrawing mid-reach, a little lonely-looking expression, gaze low, not directed at viewer, no smile, quiet unexplained restraint, illustration

## Negative Prompt

photorealistic, photo, 3d render, low quality, worst quality, bad anatomy, blurry, jpeg artifacts, harsh bright lighting, full smile, wide grin, direct frontal eye contact, sustained eye contact, overly sweet expression, cold color palette, violet tones, visible off-frame object clearly rendered, extra limbs

## Animation Instruction

Her arm lifts and extends toward the edge of frame in an unhurried reach, then stops noticeably short of whatever it was aiming for and withdraws back toward her lap. The object of the reach is never shown or clarified. Her expression stays lonely-looking and unchanged throughout, offering no explanation for the stopped gesture.

## Camera Instruction

Static extreme close-up with a slight zoom-out, framed so the off-frame target of her reach stays fully outside the shot — the camera never reveals what she almost touched.
