## Animation Instruction

Her weight shifts forward and her shoulders begin to lift as though rising from her seat, but the motion reverses partway through and she settles back down without ever standing. The faint incomplete smile she starts with fades during this reversal, leaving a neutral, unresolved expression. Her hand returns to rest near her chin by the final beat, mirroring the opening pose.

## Camera Instruction

Static extreme close-up with a slight zoom-out, held at a fixed low angle that does not tilt or rise with her partial movement — the camera stays grounded while she almost leaves.
