# Prompt 0024

## Image Prompt (KV)

masterpiece, best quality, highly detailed illustration, 1girl, solo, standing on an open rooftop terrace, bright sky with slow drifting clouds, light ocean-blue and white and silver color palette, small feather earring, natural-material accessory, light hair lifting slightly in high wind, sheer light-fabric outfit, gentle smile, soft upward gaze toward the clouds, not looking at viewer, hand raised lightly to earring, high-key daylight, airy open backdrop, clear silhouette, illustration

## Negative Prompt

photorealistic, photo, 3d, western cartoon, disney, pixar, low quality, worst quality, bad anatomy, blurry, jpeg artifacts, dark moody lighting, direct sustained eye contact, exaggerated expression, crying, hoodie, sportswear, urban clutter, night scene

## Animation Instruction

Open on an extreme close-up of her gentle smile, eyes already lifted toward the sky. Zoom out slightly to reveal the rooftop terrace setting. She raises one hand to adjust a small feather earring, a brief unhurried gesture, while her eyes continue tracking a slow-moving cloud without pause. Hand lowers by the final beat; gaze remains upward and unmoved.

## Camera Instruction

Static medium shot at a slight low angle, slow minimal zoom-out; camera stays fixed on her upward profile, never crossing into direct frontal view.
