## Animation Instruction

Her chin rests on the rabbit's head, eyes half-lowered, as her shoulders drop by a small, controlled increment — a slow exhale releasing without ever forming a smile. The motion should read as a fragile settling, not full relaxation; tension should remain faintly present in the posture. Hair and rabbit stay still; only breath and the shoulder drop carry the beat.

## Camera Instruction

Open on an extreme close-up of her chin and the rabbit's head, then ease back to a medium shot of the blanket nook; hold the camera steady and unmoving through the settling breath.
