## Animation Instruction

She exhales slowly, her visible breath blurring together with the steam rising from her mug. As the exhale finishes, her gaze drifts toward the edge of frame, toward an implied door that is never fully shown. The small incomplete smile present from the opening beat never grows further, remaining suspended and unresolved through to the final still frame.

## Camera Instruction

Static extreme close-up with a slight zoom-out, held level and unmoving, keeping the implied door just outside the frame as her gaze drifts toward it.
