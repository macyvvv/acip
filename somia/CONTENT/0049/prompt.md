## Animation Instruction

The clip opens tight on her hand around the stem, the wine already gently swirling, her observing gaze visible above the rim. As the frame widens, the swirl continues a moment longer, then her wrist stills abruptly mid-motion, the wine's surface settling flat and unmoving. The motionless hold continues through the on-screen text and the final silence, no further swirl or sip occurring.

## Camera Instruction

Static extreme close-up on the glass and lower face, easing into a slow, minimal zoom-out; keep the wine's surface clearly visible to register its stillness.
