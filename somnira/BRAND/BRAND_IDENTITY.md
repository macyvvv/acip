# Brand Identity

## Concept

Somnira is built around relational fetishism as a media concept:

- non-sexual emotional dependency
- ambiguous relationships
- unresolved presence as the core tension

The brand is about presence that feels close but never fully resolved.

## What Somnira Is Not

- Not explicit sexuality
- Not interaction dependency
- Not a conversational assistant brand
- Not a general-purpose entertainment platform

## Brand Philosophy

- Ambiguity is maintained, not explained away.
- The character relationship is the product surface.
- Emotional dependency is framed as atmosphere, not mechanics.
- Hidden backstory exists, but it is not exposed as a narrative dump.
