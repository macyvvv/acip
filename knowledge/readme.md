# Knowledge Layer

## Purpose

The Knowledge Layer stores reusable business intelligence for ACIP.

Knowledge is treated as a first-class asset and is separated from runtime, implementation, and governance.

The objective is to maximize long-term organizational intelligence.

---

## Principles

Knowledge should be:

- reusable
- version controlled
- platform independent
- machine readable
- human understandable

---

---

## Canonical Rule

Repository is the canonical source.

Conversation is never canonical.

---

## Update Policy

Knowledge should evolve continuously.

Existing knowledge should be refined instead of duplicated whenever possible.
