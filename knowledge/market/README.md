# Market Intelligence

Store market observations and business opportunities.

- What opportunities exist?
- What markets are growing?
- What trends create new value?

---

## Scope

- market trends
- category research
- demand analysis
- blue ocean opportunities
- business hypotheses

---

## Out of Scope

- prompt engineering
