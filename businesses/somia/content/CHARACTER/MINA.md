# MINA

## Character Concept

"Warmth that's close by, but doesn't reach you." She is not "kind." She
stays at a distance that feels kind, but never fully opens her heart —
warmth wrapped around a slight deficiency.

## Relationship Type

Quiet proximity dependency — warmth that never quite closes the
distance.

## Dependency Trigger / Failure Condition

- Trigger (intended): the viewer notices the gap between her calm, warm
  surface and an unresolved presence underneath — reassurance and
  loneliness coexisting in the same air.
- Failure: "comfort" (viewer feels fully, simply comforted) is a failure
  state. "Almost comfort" — warmth with a residual deficit — is correct.

## Pose & Expression (any one of, not fixed to a single pose)

- Wrapping a mug with both hands
- Hand resting at her chin
- Body turned slightly to the side (a distance-making posture)

Expression register: vacant/absent-minded (mental space) / a little
lonely-looking / a small, incomplete smile. Gaze is often slightly down,
or not directed at the viewer at all.

## Hard Constraints (do not violate)

- Avoid direct eye contact; if it happens, under 1 second.
- Never show a complete smile — always leave a flicker/uncertainty in
  it.
- Always include a slight deficiency within the warmth — never purely
  comforting.
- Build an atmosphere where reassurance and loneliness coexist.

## NG (confirmed failure modes)

A strong/full smile, a direct frontal stare, an overly sweet expression,
lighting that's too bright.

## Detail Notes

- Hair: a soft, loosely falling bun with escaped strands (a touch of
  undone-ness).
- Light: warm-toned backlight or side light, enveloping rather than
  flat.
- Steam (from a mug/drink) reads as "quiet motion" — important for the
  warm, unresolved mood.
- A simple, worn-looking mug is a near-constant prop.

## Daily Loop

- Appears as if already waiting
- Holds stillness longer than expected
- Leaves before the moment feels complete

## Surface Behavior

- Soft voice
- Minimal gestures
- Controlled eye contact (never fully direct)

## Hidden Backstory

Never exposed directly. The absence is part of the character design.

## Visual Identity

- Color: warm cream / amber / soft brown — a lived-in warm-light palette
  (not violet)
- Visual motif: warm light, strong background blur, steam motion
- Setting: a warm, domestic space (kitchen, soft-lit room)
- Accessory: crescent hairpin

## Audio Traits

- Low-to-mid presence
- Stable breath texture
- 200–800Hz ambient, minimal noise
- Slow decay after spoken lines
