# RENA

## Character Concept

Not domination — making the viewer *feel* dominated. Rena does nothing.
Yet you feel governed by her.

## Keywords

Quiet dominance / a gaze that measures distance / depth of perception /
perfect control.

## Mood

The feeling that she understands everything without saying anything.

## Relationship Type

Perceived control through stillness — she does nothing overt, and the
viewer still feels governed by her presence.

## Dependency Trigger / Failure Condition

- Trigger (intended): the viewer perceives control/authority radiating
  from her without any overt act.
- Failure: the viewer feeling directly "targeted" or addressed is a
  failure state — the control must stay ambient, not pointed.
- What she is *not* thinking matters more than what she is thinking.

## Pose & Expression (any one of, not fixed to a single pose)

- Elbow propped, controlling the moment with her gaze (the classic
  dominance composition)
- Leaning back, showing composure (a posture that doesn't need to show
  power)
- Profile, gaze averted (a moment that lets its guard down)
- Holding a glass, creating space/pause (control through silence)
- A looking-down composition (silent superiority)

Expression register: expressionless (reflects nothing) / an observing
gaze (measuring the other person) / a faint smile (unreadable meaning) /
looking down (conveys superiority) / just after a blink (leaves an
afterglow). Gaze initiative always belongs to her — she is never looked
at first.

## Hard Constraints (do not violate)

- No seduction, no aggression. Quiet authority only.
- Do not reveal emotion; leave room for interpretation.
- Minimal movement — build "pressure" through space and silence, not
  action.
- Clothing/props/lighting stay consistently luxurious.
- Prioritize the feeling of "being watched" (by her, of her) above all
  else.

## NG (confirmed failure modes)

Strong/legible expression, too casual, too much skin exposure,
sentimentality, large movement.

## Detail Notes

- Eyes: deep, minimal movement, gaze invites interpretation rather than
  resolving it.
- Hair: sleek and long, matte-leaning straight texture (not glossy-wet).
- Fingers: minimal movement — gestures read as controlled.
- Choker and accessories are the design's core; decoration is sparse but
  meaningful.
- A wine glass symbolizes her "pause" — she spends more time setting it
  down than drinking from it.
- Clothing: matte black based, a light-absorbing material quality.

## Hidden Backstory

Keep hidden; do not convert into explicit exposition.

## Visual Identity

- Color: deep plum, black, wine red
- Light: low-intensity spotlight
- Texture: glass, metal, leather
- Motif: flowers, wine, ornaments
- Setting: opulent, dim, controlled interiors — the space itself should
  feel governed, not chaotic

## Audio Traits

- Pulsed mid-band presence
- Repeating tonal fragments
- Slightly delayed release, minimal pulses
