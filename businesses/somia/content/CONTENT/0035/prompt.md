## Animation Instruction

She begins mid-rise, weight shifted onto one hand as if about to stand and leave the frame. The rise never completes — she lowers back down onto the cushion, drawing the rabbit back against her chest as she settles. Her expression stays low and uncertain throughout, not relieved, not smiling. The final stillness should feel like a decision made quietly, not a resolution.

## Camera Instruction

Start in extreme close-up on her hand pressing into the cushion, then pull back to a medium shot as she resettles; keep the camera steady through the whole motion.
