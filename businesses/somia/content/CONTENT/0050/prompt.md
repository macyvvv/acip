## Animation Instruction

She opens with chin already lowered and the faint smile in place, fingertip already resting on the glass stem. As the frame widens, the fingertip presses down a fraction, testing the stem's balance, then stills entirely without ever lifting the glass. The downward gaze and faint smile hold unchanged through the on-screen text and into the final stillness.

## Camera Instruction

Static extreme close-up angled slightly from above, easing into a slow, minimal zoom-out; keep the glass and her lowered gaze both visible without favoring either.
