## Animation Instruction

She stays turned toward the window throughout, mostly still. Partway through, a trace of rain on the glass slides slightly; her eyes track it for less than a second, a brief wavering focus, before drifting back to unfocused stillness. She never turns toward the camera. Hair remains nearly static, no wind despite the window setting. Maintain the illustrated style consistently.

## Camera Instruction

Open on an extreme close-up of her face turned toward the window, slight zoom out to reveal the room and window, then hold static, no further movement.
