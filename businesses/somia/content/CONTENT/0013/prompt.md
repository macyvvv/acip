## Animation Instruction

Partway through, the tablet screen briefly brightens; her eyes flick toward it for less than a second, then settle away again as the screen dims, her hand and posture otherwise unchanged. No eye contact is made with the camera at any point. Hair stays nearly still, no wind. Maintain the illustrated style consistently.

## Camera Instruction
