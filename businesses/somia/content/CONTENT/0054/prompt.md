## Image Prompt (KV)

## Negative Prompt

photorealistic, photo, 3d render, low quality, worst quality, bad anatomy, blurry, jpeg artifacts, harsh bright lighting, full smile, wide grin, direct frontal eye contact, sustained eye contact, overly sweet expression, cold color palette, violet tones, extra fingers, malformed hands

## Animation Instruction

One hand lifts slowly to touch the crescent hairpin and the loose strands near it, the gesture unhurried and slightly longer than a simple adjustment would need — it reads as self-soothing rather than practical. Her gaze stays low throughout, never lifting to meet the viewer. The hand lowers back near the mug by the final beat, returning to stillness without resolving her expression.

## Camera Instruction

Static extreme close-up easing to a slight zoom-out, camera held level and unmoving, letting the small hand gesture be the only motion in frame.
