# NAO Reference Sheet Transcription (somia_nao01.png, somia_nao02.png)

Full verbatim transcription of both reference sheet images, read
corner-to-corner, 2026-07-18. Written because prior passes worked from a
prose summary of these images and lost detail/nuance in translation —
this file is the actual source of record; CHARACTER.md and any content
spec should trace back to this, not to a paraphrase of it.

## Header (both sheets, identical)

- Title: "NAO / ナオ"
- Tagline: "— そこにいるだけで、世界が優しくなる。" ("Just by being there, the world becomes gentler/kinder.")
- Logo: crescent moon + "somia"
- Footer: "somia project / Character Design Rough" | "NAO / ナオ" | "2024.05"

## CONCEPT (sheet 1 only)

"光・透明感・風・海。ナオは自然と一体化している。静かで穏やかで、でも、芯は強くまっすぐ。"
(Light, transparency, wind, sea. Nao is unified with nature. Quiet and
gentle, but her core is strong and straight/unwavering.)

## KEYWORD tags (sheet 1 only)

自然との一体感 (unity with nature) / 透明感・純粋 (transparency, purity) /
風のような自由 (freedom like the wind) / 柔らかな包容力 (soft embrace/
inclusiveness) / 内にある強さ (inner strength)

## IMAGE KEY (sheet 1 only)

- 色 (color): 青水色、白、シルバー (aqua-blue, white, silver)
- 光 (light): 自然光、反射、ハイキー (natural light, reflection, high-key)
- 質感 (texture): 水滴、風に揺れる髪 (water droplets, wind-swayed hair)
- モチーフ (motif): 風、海、貝殻、羽根 (wind, sea, shells, feathers)
- 装飾品 (accessory): 天然石、薄絹素材 (natural stone, thin sheer/gauze fabric)

## MOOD (sheet 1 only)

「癒しと希望をくれる」、心が浄化されるような感覚
("Gives healing and hope" — a feeling like the heart being purified.)

## その他メモ / Other Notes (sheet 1 only, identical concept restated implicitly on sheet 2)

- ナオは「自然の化身」のような存在。(Nao is like an embodiment/incarnation of nature.)
- ストーリー上、希望や癒しの象徴として描く。(Narratively, depict her as a symbol of hope and healing.)
- 海・風・光の要素を常にデザインに取り入れる。(Always incorporate sea/wind/light elements into the design.)

## Large hero portrait (sheet 1, left panel)

Profile view, gazing upward/outward toward the sky and sea. Hair
streaming dramatically in the wind, very long, most of the visible mass
is dark navy blue with the wind-caught outer strands catching light and
reading pale blue/near-white at the tips. One hand resting lightly on
the windowsill. Loose, soft draped top — sleeves are long and full,
gathered/loose at the cuff; the neckline sits low across the collarbone
but the shoulder itself is mostly still covered by draped fabric and
hair, not a stark bare-shoulder cut. A blue teardrop earring is visible.
Sheer white curtains frame both sides of the window opening. Ocean fills
the background below a pale blue sky.

## ディテールメモ (Detail Memo, sheet 1)

- Eye close-up: "瞳は澄んだ青。光を受けると輝きを増し、吸い込まれそうな透明感。"
  (Eyes are a clear blue. They gain brilliance in light, a transparency
  you feel drawn into.)
- Ear/profile close-up (shows the earring clearly — a small teardrop-cut
  blue gem on a thin silver post/hook): "髪は風になびき、柔らかく軽やか。光が透けるような描写。"
  (Hair sways in the wind, soft and light. Rendered as if light passes
  through it.)
- Hair-flow close-up (back/side of head, no face): "風と一体になるような流れる髪の動き。自然体で飾らない。"
  (Hair movement that becomes one with the wind. Natural, unadorned.)
- Feather icon: "羽根モチーフは自由や軽やかさを表す。" (The feather motif expresses freedom and lightness.)
- Shell icon: "貝殻は海とのつながり、優しさと記憶の象徴。" (The shell symbolizes connection to the sea, gentleness and memory.)
- Pendant icon (a teardrop blue gem on a fine chain, distinct item from
  the earring): "天然石アクセサリーは癒しと守りの意味。" (The natural-stone accessory means healing and protection.)
- Sheer fabric swatch icon: "薄絹素材は光を通し柔らかな透明感を淡める。" (The thin sheer fabric lets light through, imparting a soft transparency.)

**Note: the pendant necklace and the earring are drawn as two separate
accessory items in this detail panel**, both using the same blue
teardrop gem motif — not one single item. Prior specs only ever named
"the earring."

## 表情バリエーション / Expression Variations (sheet 1) — all profile-angle, no frontal/direct-camera panel among these 5

01. 穏やかな微笑み (安心感を与える) — gentle/quiet smile; gives a sense of security/reassurance
02. 風を感じる横顔 (自由を楽しむ) — profile feeling the wind; enjoying freedom
03. 柔らかく見つめる (優しさ・包容力) — softly gazing; gentleness/embrace
04. 少し考えごと (静けさ・内省) — a little lost in thought; quietness/introspection
05. 照らされた笑顔 (希望に満ちた表情) — illuminated smile; an expression full of hope

All five are subtle, closed-mouth or barely-parted-lips smiles/looks —
none of them is a big open expression. The "笑顔" (smile) in 05 is still
gentle/luminous, not a wide grin.

## 姿勢・構図バリエーション / Pose Variations (sheet 1)

A. 窓辺に佇む (風に包まれている) — standing at the window; enveloped by wind
B. 海を見つめる横顔 (遠くを見つめる横顔) — profile gazing at the sea; gazing into the distance
C. 風に髪をなびかせる (自由で開放的) — hair streaming in the wind; free, open
D. 手を伸ばして光を受ける (希望を受け取るように) — reaching a hand out to receive light; as if receiving hope
E. 優しく微笑み振り向く (誰かを受け入れるように) — gently smiling, turning back; as if welcoming/accepting someone

## カラーイメージ (both sheets, identical 5-swatch gradient)

Dark navy blue → medium blue → light blue → pale ice blue → near-white
lavender-gray. Read as a single continuous gradient family, not five
unrelated colors — this is very likely the same gradient logic that
should apply to her hair (dark base, light tips), not a contradiction to
resolve but the palette literally showing the gradient.

## デザインポリシー / Design Policy (identical on both sheets)

- 自然光を最大限活かした透明感のある表現を優先する (Prioritize a transparent look making maximum use of natural light)
- 彩度はやや高めの青・水色・白を基調に、清潔感と爽やかさを表現 (Slightly high-saturation blue/aqua/white base, expressing cleanliness and freshness)
- 動きは「風」を意識し、柔らかく流れるラインを重視する (Motion should be conscious of "wind," emphasizing soft flowing lines)
- 過度な装飾は避け、ナチュラルで上品にまとめる (Avoid excessive ornamentation, keep it natural and refined)
- 「そこにいるだけで癒される」感覚を最優先する (Prioritize the feeling of "being healed just by her presence" above all)

## NG例 / NG Examples (sheet 1: 4 items; sheet 2: 5 items, extra "過度な装飾")

Sheet 1: 晴やぎすぎる表現 (too-cheerful/bright expression) / 重い装飾 (heavy
decoration) / 派手すぎる色使い (overly flashy coloring) / 硬い表情・不自然なポーズ
(stiff expression / unnatural pose)

Sheet 2 (same four plus): 過度な装飾 (excessive ornamentation, shown with a
potted-plant icon crossed out — i.e. don't add unrelated decorative
props to the scene)

**This is a direct, repeated warning against anything "flashy," "heavy,"
or "excessive" — directly relevant to why the redesigned prompt's dense,
heavily-qualified prose (multiple parenthetical corrections, "NOT X"
clauses) produced a bad result: it's the opposite instinct from this
sheet's own explicit design policy of restraint/subtlety.**

## ディテールメモ② (sheet 1 only, second detail row)

- Wind-blown hair strands close-up: "風で揺れる髪の細部は繊細に描き込み透明感を出す。"
  (Fine details of wind-blown hair are rendered **delicately** to bring out transparency.)
- Hands catching light/water droplets close-up: "光や水滴を受ける手元は薄く丁寧に描写する。"
  (Hands catching light/droplets are drawn **thin and careful** — delicate, not heavy-handed.)
- Fabric drape close-up: "衣類は風に揺れる薄絹素材で自然なドレープを表現。"
  (Clothing is thin sheer fabric swaying in the wind, expressing a natural drape.)
- Window/sky/sea background close-up: "背景は海や空を基調に開放感を大切にする。"
  (Background is sea/sky-based, valuing a sense of openness.)

**The word 繊細 (delicate/fine/sensitive) appears explicitly here** —
this is very likely the specific textual source of "Nao's delicacy" the
operator says has been lost in the recent renders. It is used to
describe *how the hair and hands are drawn* (fine, soft linework, not
bold/heavy), not just an abstract personality trait — a concrete art-
direction instruction as much as a character-writing one.

## 姿勢・構図バリエーション (sheet 2) — different 5 panels from sheet 1's

A. 窓辺に佇む (光を受けて見つめる) — standing at the window; gazing while receiving light
B. 海を見つめる横顔 (風に髪がなびく) — profile gazing at the sea; hair blowing in the wind
C. 振り返る (一瞬こちらを見て微笑む) — turning back; glancing this way **for just a moment** with a smile
D. 風を感じて深呼吸 (目を閉じて身を委ねる) — feeling the wind, a deep breath; eyes closed, surrendering to it
E. 座って膝を抱える (静かに考え事をしている) — sitting, hugging her knees; quietly lost in thought

**Pose C here is the single clearest reference for the "one brief
acknowledgment glance" beat already central to the script** — "一瞬" (a
single instant/moment) is explicit in the source material, not
something invented for this episode.

**Pose E (sitting, knees hugged, in thought) is a materially different
register from anything used in the current script** — a private, closed,
seated moment, more vulnerable/introspective than "standing at a window."
Not used in the current design; noting it exists as an alternate future
episode direction, not folding it into 0007's redesign now.

## Outfit — cross-checked across every panel on both sheets

Every single pose across both sheets (A-E ×2, plus the hero portrait)
shows the same basic garment: a **loose, soft, long-sleeved top/blouse**
in white-to-pale-blue tones. Sleeves are consistently long, full, and
loosely gathered — never short or fitted. The neckline sits low but is
substantially covered by hair and the garment's own draped fabric in
nearly every panel; only 1-2 panels (the hero portrait, pose D) show any
bare shoulder/collarbone, and even there it reads as *fabric having
slipped slightly*, not a tailored off-the-shoulder cut. **No panel shows
a fitted, structured, or skin-baring silhouette.** The overall read is
closer to a soft, oversized, cardigan-or-blouse-like garment than a
fitted or deliberately shoulder-baring top.

**Operator correction (2026-07-18, verbal): explicitly NOT off-shoulder
— "薄手のカーディガンのイメージ" (the image of a thin/lightweight cardigan),
avoid exposure as much as possible.** This transcription supports that
correction: the reference art's occasional shoulder-adjacent drape reads
as incidental fabric movement in a couple of panels, not a consistent,
intentional off-shoulder design choice across the sheet as a whole.
