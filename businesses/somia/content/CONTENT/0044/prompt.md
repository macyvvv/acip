## Animation Instruction

The clip opens just after a blink, the glass already held near her collarbone, the afterglow of that blink lingering in her eyes. As the frame widens she remains still, then raises the glass a further inch toward her lips — stopping short of any contact — before lowering it back to rest at the same measured pace. No sip occurs; the restraint itself is the gesture, holding through the final stillness.

## Camera Instruction

Static extreme close-up easing into a slow, minimal zoom-out; keep the glass and her expression both in frame without cutting to either alone.
