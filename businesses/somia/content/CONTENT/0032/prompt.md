## Animation Instruction

Her fingers twist the hoodie cuff in small, repeated motions, knuckles tightening. Her eyes lift briefly toward off-camera, a flicker of a glance, then drop again just as quickly — the glance never becomes a hold. The twisting fingers slow near the end but keep a light grip on the fabric rather than releasing it entirely. Rabbit and hair remain still; only hands and eyes carry the anxious beat.

## Camera Instruction

Start in extreme close-up on the twisting fingers at a low angle, then rise and pull back slightly to a medium shot; keep the camera still through the glance and its retraction.
