# Prompt 0029

## Image Prompt (KV)

masterpiece, best quality, highly detailed illustration, 1girl, solo, standing on a bright cliffside path at early dawn, pale gold and light ocean-blue and white and silver color palette, soft dawn light on skin, wind-stirred light hair, natural-material stone pendant necklace held gently in one hand, sheer light-fabric dress, calm reassuring expression, gaze fixed on the distant horizon, not looking at viewer, high-key soft dawn lighting, open sky and sea backdrop, clear silhouette, illustration

## Negative Prompt

photorealistic, photo, 3d, western cartoon, disney, pixar, low quality, worst quality, bad anatomy, blurry, jpeg artifacts, dark moody lighting, direct sustained eye contact, exaggerated expression, crying, hoodie, sportswear, night scene, harsh shadows

## Animation Instruction

Open on an extreme close-up of her calm face lit by soft dawn light. Zoom out slightly to reveal the cliffside path setting. She closes her hand gently around the stone pendant at her chest as the wind stirs her hair, her gaze remaining fixed on the horizon without wavering. Hold the closed-hand pose through the remaining beats, no further movement.

## Camera Instruction

Static medium shot at eye level with a slow, minimal zoom-out; soft warm dawn light motivates gentle highlight shifts across her face, no camera pan or dolly.
