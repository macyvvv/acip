## Animation Instruction

Her body begins already turned partway to the side. Over the middle beat she shifts further away with an unhurried, deliberate motion, weight resettling once. The small smile present at the opening fades before it ever completes, leaving an ambiguous, unresolved expression. She does not turn back or look toward the viewer at any point.

## Camera Instruction

Static extreme close-up pulling back slightly, held at a fixed low angle that never reorients to follow her turn — the camera lets her drift out of full frontal view.
