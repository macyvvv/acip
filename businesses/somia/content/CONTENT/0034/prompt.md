## Animation Instruction

She begins with the rabbit raised high enough to hide most of her face, one eye visible over its head. Her arms lower the rabbit slightly, revealing a bit more of her expression, then the motion halts partway — neither fully hidden nor fully shown. Her visible eye stays cautious throughout, never settling into a smile or a full held gaze. The pause at the end should feel suspended mid-decision.

## Camera Instruction

Open on an extreme close-up of the peeking eye at eye level, then ease back to a medium shot; keep the camera static through the partial lower and the final hold.
