## Animation Instruction

Midway through, her hand slides slowly off the mug handle and settles flat on the desk, her sleeve brushing the small plant so a single leaf trembles faintly, then everything stills again. No eye contact with camera occurs. Hair stays nearly static, no wind. Maintain the illustrated style consistently.

## Camera Instruction
