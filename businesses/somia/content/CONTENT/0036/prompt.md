## Animation Instruction

She begins pressed nearly flat against the doorframe, one eye peeking around its edge. She leans a small, deliberate amount further into the room, testing visibility, then catches herself mid-lean and retreats back to the original position by the same small margin. Her grip on the rabbit stays tight and unchanging throughout. The final position should mirror the opening pose, unresolved.

## Camera Instruction

Open on an extreme close-up of the doorframe's edge and her peeking eye, then pull back slightly to a medium shot; hold the camera steady through the lean and retreat.
