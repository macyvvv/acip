## Animation Instruction

Her lips part slightly as if a word is about to form, then press back together before any sound would emerge. Her shoulders draw inward in a small, contained motion, chin dipping lower — restrained, not a full collapse or sob. The rabbit stays fixed against her chest without shifting. The ending stillness should feel apologetic and small, not comforted or resolved.

## Camera Instruction

Open in extreme close-up on her lips and chin, then ease back to a medium shot of the folded nook posture; keep the camera locked off through the inward draw.
