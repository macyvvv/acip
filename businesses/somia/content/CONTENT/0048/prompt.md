## Animation Instruction

She opens in profile, the afterglow of a recent blink already present, beside a steady candle flame. As the frame widens, the flame flickers once, a brief shadow passing across her face — but her expression and posture remain entirely unmoved, no blink, no glance, no flinch. The unreactive stillness holds through the on-screen text and the final silence, the candle the only thing that moved.

## Camera Instruction

Static extreme close-up in profile easing into a slow, minimal zoom-out; let the candle's flicker read clearly in frame while her stillness remains the visual anchor.
