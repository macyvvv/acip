## Animation Instruction

Her hand begins drawing the hood upward in a slow, deliberate motion, as though preparing to vanish inside it. Partway through — hood only half-raised — the motion stalls completely; her hand stays pressed against the fabric, neither finishing the pull nor releasing it back down. Her gaze stays low throughout, never lifting toward the viewer. The stillness at the end should feel suspended, not settled.

## Camera Instruction

Open in extreme close-up on the hood's edge and her fingers, then pull back slowly to a medium shot of the corner nook; hold the frame steady through the stall.
