# Prompt 0052

## Image Prompt (KV)

masterpiece, best quality, highly detailed illustration, 1girl, solo, warm amber and cream and soft brown palette, softly blurred domestic room background, warm enveloping light, loosely falling bun, escaped strands framing face, crescent hairpin, hand resting lightly at chin, lips barely parted as if about to speak, a little lonely-looking expression, gaze soft and unfocused, not directed at viewer, no smile, quiet contemplative mood, warm light against an undercurrent of loneliness, illustration

## Negative Prompt

photorealistic, photo, 3d render, low quality, worst quality, bad anatomy, blurry, jpeg artifacts, bright overexposed lighting, full smile, wide grin, direct frontal eye contact, sustained eye contact, overly sweet expression, cold color palette, violet tones, extra fingers, distorted hands

## Animation Instruction

Her hand stays resting at her chin throughout. Her lips part very slightly, as though a word is about to form, then close again with no sound produced — the gesture reads as an abandoned sentence rather than a pause. Her expression stays lonely-looking and unresolved, never shifting to a smile or clear emotion, both before and after the almost-speech moment.

## Camera Instruction

Static extreme close-up drifting to a slight zoom-out at eye level, no reframing after — the camera simply watches, unhurried.
