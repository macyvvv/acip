# Prompt 0022

## Image Prompt (KV)

masterpiece, best quality, highly detailed illustration, 1girl, solo, standing at a seaside cliff edge, bright open sky and sea backdrop, light ocean-blue and white and silver color palette, sea breeze lifting long light hair, small shell pendant necklace, natural-material jewelry, sheer light fabric dress moving in wind, calm reassuring expression, gentle composure, gaze fixed on the distant horizon, not looking at viewer, hand raised lightly touching pendant, high-key natural lighting, clear silhouette against sky and sea, illustration

## Negative Prompt

photorealistic, photo, 3d, western cartoon, disney, pixar, low quality, worst quality, bad anatomy, blurry, jpeg artifacts, dark moody lighting, direct sustained eye contact, smiling widely, exaggerated expression, crying, hoodie, sportswear, urban background, night scene

## Animation Instruction

Begin on an extreme close-up of her calm, reassured face turned toward the sea. Zoom out slightly to place her at the cliff edge with wind moving her hair. She raises one hand and touches the shell pendant at her collarbone, a small, unhurried gesture, while her gaze remains locked on the horizon throughout. Hand lowers slowly by the final beat; no further motion.

## Camera Instruction

Static wide-to-medium framing at eye level with a slow, minimal zoom-out; camera never pans toward her face, holding the outward-facing composition.
