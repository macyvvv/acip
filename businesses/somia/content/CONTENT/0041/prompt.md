## Animation Instruction

She begins in extreme close-up, elbow propped, gaze already set at a measured distance — no adjustment, no reaction to the camera. As the frame widens slightly she remains fixed, then lifts the wine glass a mere inch, holding it a beat longer than expected before lowering it back to the table with the same unhurried control. No further movement follows; the hands return to stillness and hold through the final silence.

## Camera Instruction

Static extreme close-up easing into a slight, slow zoom-out; no pans, no reframing — the stillness of the camera should mirror her own restraint.
