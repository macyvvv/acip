masterpiece, best quality, highly detailed illustration, 1girl, solo, standing near open water, bright daylight, light ocean-blue and white and silver color palette, damp hair with light strands catching sunlight, single water droplet trailing down forearm, natural-material bracelet with small stone charm, sheer light wet-clinging fabric (modest, non-explicit), quiet resolute expression, calm composure, gaze directed toward distant horizon, not looking at viewer, high-key natural lighting, soft reflective water backdrop, clear silhouette, illustration

## Negative Prompt

photorealistic, photo, 3d, western cartoon, disney, pixar, low quality, worst quality, bad anatomy, blurry, jpeg artifacts, explicit content, revealing clothing, suggestive pose, dark moody lighting, direct sustained eye contact, exaggerated expression, hoodie, sportswear, night scene

## Animation Instruction

Open on an extreme close-up of her quietly resolute face with damp, sunlit hair. Zoom out slightly to reveal her standing near open water. A single droplet trails slowly down her forearm; her eyes follow it briefly, then lift back to the horizon without any change in expression. Hold the outward gaze through the remaining beats, arm settling at rest.

## Camera Instruction

Static medium shot at eye level with a slow, minimal zoom-out; brief incidental focus pull to the droplet on her arm, then returning to her face without cutting.
