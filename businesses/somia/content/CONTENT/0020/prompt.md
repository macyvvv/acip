## Animation Instruction

She stays turned toward the window throughout, eyelids heavy. Partway through, a pale reflection of the monitor light drifts slowly across the window glass as she shifts her weight slightly in her seat without standing, her gaze staying low and unfocused. She never turns toward the camera. Hair remains nearly static, no wind. Maintain the illustrated style consistently.

## Camera Instruction

Open on an extreme close-up of her tired face turned toward the window, slight zoom out to reveal the window and room, then hold static without further movement.
