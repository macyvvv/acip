## Animation Instruction

Her grip on the mug visibly tightens over the middle beat, knuckles paling slightly, while the small incomplete smile she opens with stays frozen rather than growing into a full expression. The tension reads entirely through her hands, not her face. By the final beat her grip eases and the smile fades to neutral, without ever having resolved into anything warmer.
