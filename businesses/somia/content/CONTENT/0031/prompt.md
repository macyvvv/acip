## Animation Instruction

She begins mid-blink, eyes slightly unfocused, then her eyes widen by a small, controlled degree as she registers a presence — no gasp, no flinch. Her hand, mid-adjustment on the blanket's edge, freezes rather than completing its motion. Shoulders lift a fraction and hold. She does not turn away and does not turn toward the viewer; the ambiguity is the point. Hair and rabbit stay still; only breath and the frozen hand carry the beat.

## Camera Instruction

Open on an extreme close-up of her eyes at eye level, then ease back to a medium shot with a slow, minimal dolly-out; hold static through the freeze and the stillness.
