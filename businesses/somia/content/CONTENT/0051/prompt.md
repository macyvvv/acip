## Animation Instruction

She holds her mug with both hands as steam rises in a slow, continuous curl. Her gaze does not lift; her expression stays vacant and unfocused throughout, never resolving into a smile or a frown. The stillness extends noticeably past the point where a viewer expects her to move, drink, or look up — she simply doesn't. By the final beat the steam thins and her posture has not shifted at all.

## Camera Instruction

Static extreme close-up easing into a slight zoom-out, held at a low, intimate eye level with no further movement — quiet and unhurried throughout.
