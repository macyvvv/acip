## Animation Instruction

Her foot shifts slightly, testing weight toward an unseen exit. Her shoulder begins to rotate in that direction, a small preparatory turn, then stalls before committing — her weight settles back against the wall, body left half-turned. The rabbit swings only slightly with the motion, then stills. The final pose should look genuinely undecided, neither leaving nor fully staying.

## Camera Instruction

Start in extreme close-up on the shifting foot at floor level, then rise and pull back to a medium shot against the wall; keep the camera static through the stalled turn.
