# Prompt 0030

## Image Prompt (KV)

masterpiece, best quality, highly detailed illustration, 1girl, solo, standing at a cliffside overlook above breaking waves, bright coastal daylight, light ocean-blue and white and silver color palette, fine sea spray catching light on skin, wind-blown light hair, natural-material shell hair clip, sheer light-fabric dress, gentle smile, eyes closed transitioning to softly open, gaze toward the distant horizon, not looking at viewer, high-key natural lighting, dramatic open sky and sea backdrop, clear silhouette, illustration

## Negative Prompt

photorealistic, photo, 3d, western cartoon, disney, pixar, low quality, worst quality, bad anatomy, blurry, jpeg artifacts, dark moody lighting, direct sustained eye contact, exaggerated expression, crying, hoodie, sportswear, night scene

## Animation Instruction

Open on an extreme close-up of her gentle smile as fine sea spray catches the light on her skin. Zoom out slightly to reveal the cliffside overlook. A gust carries spray across her face; she closes her eyes briefly against it, then slowly reopens them toward the horizon. Hold the reopened, outward gaze through the remaining beats without further movement.

## Camera Instruction

Static wide-to-medium shot at eye level with a slow, minimal zoom-out; no pan or dolly, holding the outward-facing composition through the gust and stillness.
