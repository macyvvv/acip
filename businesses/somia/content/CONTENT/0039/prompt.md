## Animation Instruction

Her hand moves slowly and deliberately toward the rabbit's ear, fingertips approaching at an unhurried pace. Just before contact, the motion halts entirely — the hand hovers, suspended, without touching. Her gaze stays unfocused and inward throughout, never settling on the rabbit or the viewer. The final frame should hold the hover, contact still undecided.

## Camera Instruction

Open on an extreme close-up of the hovering fingertips near the rabbit's ear, then pull back gently to a medium shot of her curled form; hold the camera still through the entire hover.
