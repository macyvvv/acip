# Prompt 0028

## Image Prompt (KV)

masterpiece, best quality, highly detailed illustration, 1girl, solo, standing at a tall open window overlooking sky and sea, bright high-key daylight, light ocean-blue and white and silver color palette, distant seabird in flight visible through the window, natural-material bracelet with feather charm, sheer light-fabric dress, quiet resolute expression, gaze directed outward and slightly upward following the bird, not looking at viewer, soft natural window light, airy open backdrop, clear silhouette, illustration

## Negative Prompt

photorealistic, photo, 3d, western cartoon, disney, pixar, low quality, worst quality, bad anatomy, blurry, jpeg artifacts, dark moody lighting, direct sustained eye contact, exaggerated expression, crying, hoodie, sportswear, cluttered interior, night scene

## Animation Instruction

Open on an extreme close-up of her quietly resolute face, gaze already fixed outward. Zoom out slightly to reveal the tall open window setting. A seabird crosses her line of sight; her head turns slightly, tracking it for a moment, then returns smoothly to its original outward position. Hold the final still gaze through the remaining beats.

## Camera Instruction

Static medium shot at eye level with a slow, minimal zoom-out; camera holds through the head turn without following the bird itself, keeping her as the constant subject.
