## Animation Instruction

She remains curled with her cheek against her knee, eyelids heavy throughout. Around the middle of the clip, the tablet screen flickers once with a brief, illegible notification glow; in response she only shifts her weight slightly, her sleeve slipping a little further off her shoulder, without turning to look at it. No eye contact with camera is made. Hair and fabric move minimally, no wind. Keep the illustrated style consistent; no photorealistic drift.

## Camera Instruction

Start on an extreme close-up of her face at knee height, then a slow, minimal zoom out to reveal her curled posture; hold static for the remainder, no further camera movement.
